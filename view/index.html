Introduction

You can search the tasklist for feature-requests and bugs. When you register and login, you are allowed to open new tasks, submit feature requests and write comments yourself. Once the site admins added you to the assignees list, you can assign tasks to yourself (or tasks might be assigned to you by the admins) and change the status of tasks assigned to you.
